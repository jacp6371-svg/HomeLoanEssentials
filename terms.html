6. How We Are Paid — Commission Disclosure

Home Loan Essentials does not charge clients a fee for standard mortgage broking services. We are remunerated by lenders through commissions, which may include:

Upfront Commission

A one-time payment from the lender, typically calculated as a percentage of the loan amount settled (generally 0.5%–0.7% + GST).

Trail Commission

An ongoing payment from the lender for the life of the loan, typically calculated as a percentage of the outstanding loan balance (generally 0.1%–0.2% + GST per annum).

These commissions are paid by lenders and do not directly affect the interest rate or fees you pay.
